Most bakeries need 24–48 hours. For custom wedding or event cakes, we recommend 1–2 weeks in advance.

Can I add a personalised message or design?

Yes — you can include text, photos, or logos. Each bakery confirms what's possible for your order.

What about dietary requirements?

Use the filter to find bakeries offering gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, or nut-free options.

What payment methods are accepted?

Visa, Mastercard, Klarna, Vipps, and corporate invoice. All payments are fully secure.
